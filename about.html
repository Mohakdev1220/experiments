About Me

I'm Mohak Choubey, a developer and educator based in Indore, India. I build experimental web projects exploring interactive design, creative coding, and emerging technologies. My work bridges the gap between education and innovation.

What I Do

I specialize in building full-featured web applications using vanilla JavaScript, focusing on performance and user experience. My projects span productivity tools, educational content, real-time applications, and interactive visualizations.

I also support educational initiatives by creating learning materials and interactive study tools for schools in Indore, bringing technology into the classroom.

Philosophy

I call myself a "vibe coder" — I believe in building things that feel good to use. Clean architecture, smooth animations, and thoughtful interactions are core to everything I create. I prefer direct, efficient collaboration and believe in learning by doing.
